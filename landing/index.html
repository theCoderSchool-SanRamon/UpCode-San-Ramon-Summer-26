The toolkit

Not just a map. A full workspace.

Every tool you'd otherwise piece together from six different sites, built to work off the same data.

Multi-mode heat map

Every county colored the way that matters to you: Price-to-Rent Ratio, Investment Score, or Population. Swap layers with one click.

City-level drill-down

An independent city layer sits on top of the county map, hoverable and toggled on its own, so you can zoom in past the county line.

Investment score & leaderboard

Six factors blended into one 0–100 rank for every county. Sort any column, click a row to fly the map straight there.

Custom weights

Care more about cash flow than appreciation? Set your own weights per factor. They have to sum to 1.00, so the score always means something.

How it works

From "somewhere in the US" to a decision.

Six steps, and you never have to open another tab.

1

Search or browse

Type a county, city, or ZIP, or just pan and zoom the map yourself. Either way, you land on the heat map.

2

Read the leaderboard

See every county ranked 0–100, weighted by the six factors you care about, sorted however you like.

3

Open the detail breakdown

Click a county or city and a radar chart pops up, a visual read of all six factors at once, not just a row of numbers.

4

Drill into a city

Zoom past the county line into the independent city layer to narrow the search even further.

5

Click a listing

For-sale listings appear on the map at high zoom with price tags. Click one to jump straight into the deal analyzer.

6

Compare up to 4

Stack properties side by side and get a plain-English verdict on which one actually wins, and why.

Data & trust

Real data. Editable assumptions. No black box.

Every score traces back to a real number, and every number is one you can override.

Official Census data

County population, rent, and demographic figures come from the US Census Bureau's American Community Survey, the same dataset economists and lenders rely on.

ACS 5-Year Estimates

Real property & listing data

Property details, tax history, and for-sale listings are pulled from RentCast, not scraped or hand-estimated.

RentCast API

Editable, analysis-only

Price, rent, tax, insurance, mortgage terms. Every input is yours to adjust. This is decision support, not financial advice.

You're in control

Analysis only. RealDeal surfaces public data and your own assumptions to help you narrow down where to look. It doesn't replace a lender, an inspector, or your own due diligence.
